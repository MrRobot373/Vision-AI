How the App Works

Step 1

Click on the Signin button.

Step 2

Sign In through your google account.

Step 3

The camera will ask for permission. After that you can capture the image through center button. it capture an image of the object or scene around you.

Step 4

The leftmost button is to flip the camera. The rightmost button is to import image from gallery.

Step 5

After capturing the image a text box will be shown below in which you can ask questions regarding the image.

Step 6

The processing time is approximately 1 mins, after which the response will stream in and can be read.
